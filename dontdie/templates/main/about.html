About Don't Die

Our Mission

Don't Die is a social platform designed to help people share meaningful messages with their friends and family - the things they'd want them to know if they were no longer here. Our mission is to create a space for authentic connection and to remind people how much they matter.

Why We Created This

Too often, we save our most heartfelt words for eulogies - when the person who needs to hear them most is gone. Don't Die flips this concept by encouraging "pre-eulogies" - tributes shared during life, when they can truly make a difference.

When someone is struggling with depression or thoughts of suicide, knowing how valued they are by their loved ones can provide critical perspective and hope. By reading tributes from friends and family when they're feeling low, people can be reminded of their importance and the meaningful connections in their life.

Our Values

Authenticity

No anonymous accounts. Real people sharing real feelings.

Meaningful Connection

Focus on quality connections rather than quantity of interactions.

Privacy & Control

Users choose when their tributes become visible to recipients.

Need Support?

If you're experiencing thoughts of suicide or need immediate support, please reach out for help:

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 1-800-273-8255
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • International Association for Suicide Prevention: Find a crisis center near you

Remember: You matter. You are valued. And there are people who care about you.
