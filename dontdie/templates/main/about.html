About Don't Die

Our Mission

Don't Die is a platform designed to help people express appreciation for their loved ones while they're still here.

Too often, we wait until someone is gone to share how much they meant to us. At funerals and memorials, people share beautiful sentiments that the deceased never got to hear. Don't Die aims to change that.

By creating a space where people can write tributes to their friends and family members, we hope to foster deeper connections and help people understand their positive impact on others' lives.

Express Appreciation

Tell people how much they mean to you while they can still hear it. Share stories, memories, and the impact they've had on your life.

Prevent Suicide

For some, knowing they're valued can be life-saving. Your words of appreciation could help someone through a dark time.

Build Connection

Strengthen your relationships by sharing meaningful exchanges of gratitude and appreciation.
