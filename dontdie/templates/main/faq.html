Frequently Asked Questions

Don't Die is a social platform designed to help people share meaningful messages with their friends and family - the things they'd want them to know if they were no longer here. It's a space for authentic connection and to remind people how much they matter.

We call these messages "tributes" - they're like pre-eulogies that can be shared with people while they're still alive, when the words can truly make a difference.

Too often, we save our most heartfelt words for eulogies - when the person who needs to hear them most is gone. Don't Die flips this concept by encouraging people to share these meaningful messages during life.

When someone is struggling with depression or thoughts of suicide, knowing how valued they are by their loved ones can provide critical perspective and hope.

Even for those not experiencing crisis, receiving the gift of knowing how much you matter to someone can be profoundly moving and strengthen your relationships.

  1. Create an account - Sign up with your real identity. This platform is about authenticity.
  2. Connect with friends - Find and add friends and family members you care about.
  3. Write tributes - Share what you appreciate about them - what you'd want them to know if they were gone.
  4. Control visibility - You can keep tributes private until you're ready to share them, or make them visible right away.
